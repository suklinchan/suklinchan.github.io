How Driving Behaviors Contribute to Fatal Collisions?

It'd be interesting to find out how the different types of negligent driving behaviors correlate with the total number of collisions. We want to explore questions like: Which behavior could be a good predictor of a driver getting involved in crashes again? For states with similiar number of total crashes, which one has the worst drivers in terms of a particular irresponsible behavior?

Use the dropdown menu to select any of the driving behaviors, and see how they relate to the total number of collisions. What are your observations on:

  • Which driving behavior(s) exhibit a strong linear relationship with the total number of collisions.
  • Which driving behavior(s) contribute the least to the overall number of collisions.
  • Which states have the highest number of total collisions (more than 23 per billion miles), and of these, which has the worst drivers in terms of speeding.

Have fun exploring!
